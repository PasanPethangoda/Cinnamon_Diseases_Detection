About Us

Welcome to Dr. CINNAMON, your trusted partner in maintaining healthy cinnamon crops in Sri Lanka. Using advanced AI technology, we specialize in detecting and diagnosing cinnamon stem and branch diseases like Rough Bark and Stripe Canker. Our comprehensive solution includes an AI-based disease detection service and an online fertilizer store, helping planters identify and treat diseases while ensuring their crops receive essential nutrients. We're committed to enhancing the cinnamon industry by blending technology with traditional farming practices.
